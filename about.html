GIFT.TY

At Gift.ty, we believe that no special moment should go unnoticed. As a team of passionate 3rd-year Computer Engineering students, we’ve created a smart reminder and gifting app designed to help you celebrate your loved ones effortlessly.

Our mission is simple: never miss a special occasion again. Whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, or any milestone, Gift.ty keeps track of important dates and ensures your loved ones receive thoughtful, personalized gifts—delivered right on time.

With a seamless subscription-based service, we take the stress out of remembering and finding the perfect gift. From smart reminder, curated gifts to personalized greetings, we make every celebration meaningful and hassle-free.

Join us in making every occasion unforgettable—because the best gifts are the ones that come from the heart, at just the right moment.
